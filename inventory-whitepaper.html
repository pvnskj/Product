Operating model

SenseCollect telemetry, POS, supplier, and forecast signals; cleanse and dedupe.
ForecastBlend statistical models with events (weather, promos, outages).
PromiseReserve inventory with rule-based substitutions and confidence scoring.
RebalanceOptimize stocking, transfers, and buys with scenario sandboxes.
AssureMonitor SLAs, aging, and anomalies; trigger playbooks.

Data contracts

Signals

  • Availability with source lineage, freshness, and confidence.
  • Lead times with variability bands and supplier reliability.
  • Capacity (labor, dock, carrier) with constraint windows.

Quality rules

  • Anomaly detection on demand spikes and sensor noise.
  • Schema governance for UOM, pack sizes, and channel overrides.
  • Temporal joins to align signals for promise calculations.

Audit & lineage

  • Decision logs show why a node or substitute was chosen.
  • Playbook evidence stored with user, model version, and thresholds.
  • What-if runs tagged to prevent cross-contamination with prod.

Playbooks

Campaign launch

  • Simulate uplift; pre-position stock; protect critical services.
  • Expose delivery confidence and substitutes in the UX.

Disruption response

  • Auto-reroute promises when a node, lane, or supplier fails.
  • Escalate only when risk exceeds margin or service guardrails.

Lifecycle optimization

  • Phase out aging SKUs with substitution trees and markdown guidance.
  • Balance carbon and cost by steering to optimal fulfillment nodes.
