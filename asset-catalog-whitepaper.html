Operating model

AuthorGuided templates enforce taxonomy, UOM, hazard flags, and supplier metadata.
ValidateAutomated checks for duplicates, banned materials, and regional restrictions.
ApproveWorkflow routes to safety, finance, and engineering with SLA timers.
PublishAPIs distribute records to planning, fulfillment, billing, and support.
MonitorSignals on usage, substitutions, and incidents trigger playbooks.

Data contracts

Required attributes

  • Global IDs, version, lifecycle state, and effective dates.
  • Safety + compliance markers, environmental handling.
  • Compatibility and substitution matrices with confidence.

Quality rules

  • Freshness thresholds on supplier + catalog sources.
  • Dedupe similarity thresholds with human-in-loop overrides.
  • Schema evolution published with deprecation guidance.

Lineage + audit

  • Trace each attribute to source, steward, and approval.
  • Immutable change log with before/after payloads.
  • Evidence bundles exportable for auditors.

Playbooks

New product introduction

  • Author once → auto-propagate to commerce, planning, and finance.
  • Auto-derive substitutes and kits; warn on missing regulatory info.

Quality incident

  • Freeze affected items; push holds to warehouses and service teams.
  • Link replacements and automatically notify impacted orders.

Lifecycle refresh

  • Scheduled reviews on aging parts with supplier scorecards.
  • Retire + redirect logic to prevent dead references in systems.
