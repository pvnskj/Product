Data contracts

Order inputs

  • Catalog IDs with substitution trees and compliance flags.
  • Availability + freshness; capacity by node and carrier.
  • Customer constraints (delivery window, access, service needs).

Execution signals

  • Pick/pack scans with location, timestamp, and handler.
  • Photo + seal verification tied to shipment IDs.
  • Exception codes mapped to playbooks and customer messaging.

Assurance

  • Decision logs on why a promise was accepted, rerouted, or split.
  • Customer comms synchronized with operational state.
  • Settlement rules with tax, credits, and SLA adherence.

Playbooks

High-value orders

  • Two-person verification, photos, and route locks before pickup.
  • Real-time customer ETA sharing with secure proof of delivery.

Split shipments

  • Optimize across nodes while keeping delivery windows aligned.
  • Notify customer which items ship together and why.

Service + product

  • Bundle installations with hardware; verify certifications and tools.
  • Trigger billing only after service proof and asset activation.
