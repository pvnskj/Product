Blueprint

Risk-led

Critical assets prioritized by severity, exposure, and history.

Evidence rich

Media, sensors, and checklists captured with context, lineage, and signatures.

Closed loop

Exceptions route to disposition, repair, or learning with SLA timers.

Operating model

PlanCadences set by regulation, usage, and risk; dynamic routes with site constraints.
StagePeople, certifications, tools, and parts reserved with proof of readiness.
ExecuteGuided workflows capture readings, anomalies, and media with offline support.
DecideAuto-classify results; trigger holds, repairs, or coaching.
ReportEvidence bundles and KPIs pushed to regulators, customers, and leadership.

Data contracts

Signals

  • Asset identity with location, version, and calibration state.
  • Checklists with rule versions, tolerances, and instructions.
  • Media + sensor data with timestamps and geo signatures.

Quality rules

  • On-device validations (range, completeness, photo clarity).
  • Required evidence by risk level and jurisdiction.
  • Drift detection on recurring inspections and inspector variance.

Audit & lineage

  • Immutable logs of who, when, where, and which rule set.
  • Chain-of-custody for parts and tools used during the visit.
  • Redaction + retention controls aligned to regulatory windows.

Playbooks

Critical outage

  • Auto-assign crews nearest to the incident with ready kits.
  • Live evidence streamed to command for approvals and detours.

Quality escape

  • Containment holds triggered on affected batches/assets.
  • Root cause mapped to process gaps; coaching flows scheduled.

Audit request

  • One-click export of evidence bundles with signatures.
  • Reviewer portals show lineage, exceptions, and remediation.
