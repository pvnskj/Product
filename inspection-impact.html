Scenario boards

Critical infrastructure

  • Inspections coordinate with grid or utility outages to minimize downtime.
  • Parts and tools staged with chain-of-custody and calibration proof.
  • Fallback plans show detours when access or permits change last minute.

Manufacturing

  • Digital twins map defects to upstream batches for rapid containment.
  • Visual cues and AR overlays ensure every torque, seal, and test is done.
  • Nonconformances route instantly to disposition with automated holds.

Regulatory audits

  • Evidence bundles export with signatures, GPS, and timestamps.
  • Policy changes publish to checklists instantly with backward traceability.
  • Variance trends surface coaching needs and systemic design fixes.
